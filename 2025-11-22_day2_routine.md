# Day 2: Upper Body & Cardio
**Session**: Week 1, Day 2
**Duration**: 40 minutes
**Equipment**: Kettlebell (6kg), Discs (5kg), Jump rope

---

## Warm-up (5 minutes)
- Light jump rope (2 min)
- Arm circles forward & backward (1 min)
- Shoulder rolls (30 sec)
- Torso twists (1 min)
- Cat-cow stretches (30 sec)

---

## Circuit 1 (10 minutes)
**2 rounds | 40 sec work / 20 sec rest per exercise | 60 sec rest after round 2**

1. **Push-ups** (modify as needed)
2. **Kettlebell Rows** (6kg KB, bent-over, single-arm) - alternate arms each round
3. **Jump Rope** (regular pace)

---

## Circuit 2 (10 minutes)
**2 rounds | 40 sec work / 20 sec rest per exercise | 60 sec rest after round 2**

1. **Overhead Press** (6kg KB)
2. **Bent-over Rows** (5kg discs, both arms)
3. **Mountain Climbers**

---

## Circuit 3 (10 minutes)
**2 rounds | 40 sec work / 20 sec rest per exercise | 60 sec rest after round 2**

1. **Bicep Curls** (6kg KB)
2. **Tricep Dips**
3. **Jump Rope** (high knees variation)

---

## Cool-down (5 minutes)
- Walking in place (1 min)
- Shoulder stretches (1 min)
- Tricep stretches (1 min)
- Chest stretches (1 min)
- Neck rolls & upper back stretches (1 min)

---

**Notes:**
- Focus on proper form over speed
- Keep rest periods strict for maximum effectiveness
- Stay hydrated throughout the session
