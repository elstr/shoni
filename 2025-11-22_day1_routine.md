# Day 1: Lower Body & Cardio
**Session**: Week 1, Day 1
**Duration**: 40 minutes
**Equipment**: Kettlebell (6kg), Discs (5kg), Jump rope

---

## Warm-up (5 minutes)
- Light jump rope (2 min)
- Bodyweight squats (1 min)
- Leg swings - front/back & side-to-side (1 min)
- Hip circles & ankle rolls (1 min)

---

## Circuit 1 (10 minutes)
**2 rounds | 40 sec work / 20 sec rest per exercise | 60 sec rest after round 2**

1. **Goblet Squats** (6kg KB)
2. **Jump Rope** (regular pace)
3. **Weighted Glute Bridges** (5kg disc on hips)

---

## Circuit 2 (10 minutes)
**2 rounds | 40 sec work / 20 sec rest per exercise | 60 sec rest after round 2**

1. **Kettlebell Swings** (6kg KB)
2. **Weighted Bulgarian Split Squats** (5kg disc) - alternate legs each round
3. **Jump Squats**

---

## Circuit 3 (10 minutes)
**2 rounds | 40 sec work / 20 sec rest per exercise | 60 sec rest after round 2**

1. **Single-leg Deadlifts** (6kg KB) - alternate legs each round
2. **Weighted Step-ups** (5kg disc held at chest) - alternate legs
3. **Jump Rope** (high knees variation)

---

## Cool-down (5 minutes)
- Walking in place (1 min)
- Quad stretches (1 min)
- Hamstring stretches (1 min)
- Glute & hip stretches (1 min)
- Standing calf stretches (1 min)

---

**Notes:**
- Focus on proper form over speed
- Keep rest periods strict for maximum effectiveness
- Stay hydrated throughout the session
